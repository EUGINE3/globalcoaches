Integrated Module Approach

Instead of separating into 7 different categories, we have blended them into modules that cut across all levels

Module 1: Young Theologians and Gospel Advancing

Deepen theological understanding and develop transformational church leadership skills rooted in biblical principles.

Module 2: Missional Leadership

Develop missional leadership competencies that integrate faith, purpose, and strategic thinking.

Module 3: SDGs & Community Transformation

Master the UN Sustainable Development Goals framework and drive meaningful community transformation.

Module 4: Education for Sustainable Development & Global Citizenship

Become an educator and advocate for sustainable development, fostering global citizenship.

Module 5: Entrepreneurship and Political Economics

Launch and scale social enterprises while understanding political economy dynamics.

Module 6: Diplomacy & Global Leadership

Develop diplomatic skills and global leadership competencies for international relations.

Module 7: Climate Warriors

Become a climate action leader with expertise in environmental science and policy advocacy.

Each level (Certificate, Diploma, Advanced Diploma) builds on these modules with increasing depth and practice.
